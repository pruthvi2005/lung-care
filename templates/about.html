About Our Lung Cancer Prediction Tool

Empowering individuals with knowledge about their lung health through advanced machine learning.

Project Needs

  • Early detection of potential lung cancer risk factors through user inputs
  • User-friendly interface for easy data entry and result interpretation
  • Secure storage of user assessment history
  • Accurate risk prediction using machine learning algorithms
  • Responsive design for accessibility across devices
  • Clear visualization of risk assessment results
  • Educational resources about lung health and risk factors
